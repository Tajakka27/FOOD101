Club Sandwich

(18 reviews)

Club Sandwich – It is a quick and yummy fast food recipe. This recipe is nutritious, perfect for lunch boxes and delicious. How simple, beautiful and delicious is it?

Recipe

Combine mayonnaise and mustard in a small bowl; mix well. Spread 1 tsp. of this mixture on 1 side of each toast slice. To assemble each sandwich, layer 1 slice of toast, mayonnaise mixture-side up, 1 lettuce leaf, 1 slice of cheese, 2 ounces of ham (or chicken), 2 slices of tomato, 1 slice of toast, mayonnaise mixture-side down. Spread 1 tsp. of the mayonnaise mixture on toast. Continue assembling each sandwich with 1 slice of cheese, 2 ounces of turkey, 1 lettuce leaf and 1 slice of toast, mayonnaise mixture-side down. Cut into triangles. Secure with toothpicks and serve.
